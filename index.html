Details

We proudly announce the third incarnation of the PREEMPT_RT community event - what we are now calling A Summit On A Summit 2019!

The goal of this meetup is to discuss the hot topics in Linux RT in a cool and relaxed setting with spectacular views in the Austrian alps.

The conference is organized as unconference style. Although we highly appreciate if you want to give a prepared talk.

This event is organized by Christoph Hellwig, Richard Weinberger and Daniel Wagner.

Find details of previous events here: 2016, 2017

Location

The even takes place from 2019-05-31 to 2019-06-02 at the Glungezer Hütte (lodge) near Innsbruck, Tyrol, Austria. The lodge is reachable only by foot.

And here is a webcam showing the current weather situation.

In case the weather or snow condition do not allow us to go the logde we stay in Innsbruck. We will arrange the hotel and meeting location if that is the case.

Travel

The closest airport is Innsbruck. Another option is the airport in Munich which is approx. 2 hours by car from Innsbruck.

Hotels

Since the lodge is a few hours by foot from civilization, staying at the lodge strongly recommended. ;)

First and most important: yes there is power available and we will have a WiFi hotspot. It will not be fast, but checking for some emails it should be okay.

You should have a pair of good hiking shoes (This is really important!). It is not a difficult path just in case the weather is nasty. Also a rain jacket is thing to bring with you.

It also a good idea to bring your own sleeping bag. If you don't have one it is not a problem. There are blankets available at the lodge but usually they are not washed after each usage...

A hiking backpack should be enough to bring cloths and other equipment with you for 3 days.

Food and drinks are available at the lodge. The hike to the lodge is around 2 hours (7 km). So it is not particular long but you might want to bring some drinking or food with you.
